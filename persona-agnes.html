Persona

Name

Agnes

Technology

Devices

  • Laptop
  • Phone

Love/Hate

  • Loves games, especially tabletop, loves connecting with groups and introducing new games to people
  • Loves games that can have a moderately sized group, prefers collaborative games like Arkham Horror and Pandemic where players work together to solve a common problem.
  • Hates not having accessible or consistent areas to play that meet the group’s needs
  • Hates disorganization

Proficiency

  • Handles communication between friends and group chats
  • Comfortable navigating many different communication apps
  • Communicates with local venues and arranges time for the group to meet
  • Uses their laptop for most tasks, has information synced across all devices for quick and easy access
  • Confident and willing to mediate multiple people’s ideas, opinions, and concerns
  • Organized

Attitudes and Behaviors

  • Funds kickstarter games when she can
  • Supports small developers by engaging on their social media and sharing their process
  • Follows local venues and stays up to date with their event calendars
  • Attends game nights at local game and hobby stores
  • Follows other local meetup groups and hosts
  • Wants to connect people with opportunities to explore and grow
  • Curious about developing her own game, wants to find other people to work with
  • Has apps for tracking scores and general game stats
  • Loves teaching and keeping people engaged in a game

User Story

  • As a host for a local game group, I want to create maintain a well organized group that lets people find local places to meet up and play the games they're interested in.

Use Case

Agnes and her friends were hanging out and talking about wanting to find new games to play with more people. They agreed that plenty of sources already had a way to find new games, but wished there was a more convenient way to choose a game to play, and find people nearby to play it with. Agnes decided to look for a website that could help her discover games and create a group to help people interested in the same games meet up at local venues.

  • Pre-Condition: Agnes is logged into her account and on the "groups" page.
  • Post-Condition: Agnes creates a new group with its own description and preferences that allows new members to join.

Interaction-Flow/User-flow

  • Agnes uses navigational tool (nav bar) to find the site’s different pages
  • The site directs her to the “groups” page
  • Agnes is able to see and join pre-existing groups, as well as an option to make a new group, which she clicks on
  • Site redirects her to group creation page (or UI popup)
  • Agnes enters all relevant information (name of group, groups goals, member cap, location, interests, preferences) and clicks submit
  • The site stores and publishes her information so her new group can be seen on the site
  • New members can join, Agnes has permission from the site to moderate the group
